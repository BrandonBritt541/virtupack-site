The Problem

Traditional packaging validation is slow, expensive, and iterative in the worst way — every design change means a new physical prototype, lab test, and weeks of waiting. For manufacturers shipping complex or high-value products, this cycle eats time and budget before a single box ships.

The Solution

VIRTUPACK is building the platform that changes that. Upload your product's CAD file, design your packaging, and run physics-based simulations — drop, vibration, compression — in minutes instead of weeks. No prototypes. No lab time. No guessing.

CAD-Driven Accuracy

Built around real engineering data, not scans or approximations.

Full Pack-Out Simulation

Not just the box, but the complete internal packaging system.

Physics-Based Results

FEA-grade simulation that gives confidence scores, failure points, and actionable next steps.
